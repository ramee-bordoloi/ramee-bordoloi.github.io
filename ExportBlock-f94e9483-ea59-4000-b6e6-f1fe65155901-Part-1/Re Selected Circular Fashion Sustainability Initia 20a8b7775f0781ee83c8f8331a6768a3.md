# Re:Selected: Circular Fashion Sustainability Initiative

Category: Mobile Design, Responsive Design, Web Design
My Role: Product Design, Research, UI Design, UX Design, UX Writing

## Project Overview

SELECTED launched Re:Selected to support its circular fashion commitment. I led the end to end UX and UI for this re-commerce platform, enabling users to resell previously purchased SELECTED garments in exchange for vouchers, reducing fashion’s environmental impact.

## Why Re:Selected?

Fashion resale is more than a trend. It’s a response to climate concerns and consumer demand.

- Consumers are embracing secondhand amid inflation and sustainability awareness.
- Retailers are adopting resale at a faster rate to remain relevant.
- The global resale market is projected to reach $350B by 2027.

🔗 [Source: ThredUp 2023 Resale Report](https://cf-assets-tup.thredup.com/resale_report/2023/thredUP_2023_Resale%20Report_Executive%20Summary.pdf)

## Business Goals

- Show action on Bestseller’s circularity pledge.
- Reduce textile waste.
- Create a resale model scalable across markets and categories.

- Clear voucher redemption process.
- Seamless in store purchase verification.
- Trust in shipping and return.
