Re:Selected: Circular Fashion
Sustainability Initiative

My Role: Product Design  ·  Research  ·  UI Design  ·  UX Design  ·  UX Writing   |   Highlighted in: Bestseller Annual Report 2024

Project Overview

SELECTED launched Re:Selected to support its circular fashion commitment. I led the end-to-end UX and UI for this re-commerce platform, enabling users to resell previously purchased SELECTED garments in exchange for vouchers — reducing fashion's environmental impact.

Why Re:Selected?

Fashion resale is more than a trend. It's a response to real climate concerns and evolving consumer demand:

  • Consumers are embracing secondhand amid inflation and sustainability awareness.
  • Retailers are adopting resale at a faster rate to remain relevant and differentiated.
  • The global resale market is projected to reach $350 billion by 2027.

Business Goals

  • Demonstrate action on Bestseller's circularity pledge.
  • Reduce textile waste by keeping garments in circulation longer.
  • Create a resale model scalable across markets and product categories.

Research & Insights

Conducted competitive benchmarking across leading re-commerce brands including Moves, Modstrom, Lala Berlin, Markberg, and Soulland — auditing their navigation, filtering, product tile patterns, and buy-back category structures.

Core user needs identified through research:

  • Easy trade-in eligibility check — users need to know what qualifies quickly.
  • Clear voucher redemption process — transparent value exchange builds trust.
  • Seamless in-store purchase verification — must work even without a receipt.
  • Trust in shipping and return process — reduces anxiety around sending items away.

MVP Design

1. Preloved Landing Page

Messaging aligned with SELECTED's tone, offering a soft introduction to secondhand with clear next steps and a frictionless path to trade-in.

2. Trade-In Flow

Designed to be frictionless, whether or not users still have a receipt.

The trade-in journey covers four clear steps:

1
Select the trade-in item from eligible categories.
2
Confirm purchase info (with manual entry option for no-receipt users).
3
Prepare the item for shipment with clear packing guidance.
4
Receive voucher upon inspection and approval.

Outcomes

  • Launched MVP for the Denmark market.
  • Received positive stakeholder feedback on clarity, simplicity, and alignment with SELECTED's brand tone.
  • Established a scalable framework for future phases across additional markets and categories.
  • Project highlighted in the Bestseller Annual Report 2024.
