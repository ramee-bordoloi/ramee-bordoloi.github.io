# Launching Loyalty: Omnichannel Rewards Program

Category: Mobile Design, Responsive Design, Web Design
My Role: Product Design, Research, UI Design, UX Design, UX Writing

## Overview

Led the end to end design of an omni channel loyalty program that unifies retail and online shopping for **3+ fashion brands across 13 European markets**. Delivered a single member experience on web, app, and POS that increased sign ups and  incremental revenue in its first post launch quarter.

## Why did we do it?

We needed to replace fragmented brand clubs with a unified loyalty platform to

- Increase customer lifetime value and repeat purchases
- Capture GDPR compliant, cross channel first party data
- Support the company’s Unified Commerce strategy
- Differentiate our brands in a crowded fashion market

## Process

1. Discover requirements with business, brand, and internal and external engineering, CRM and retail stakeholders
2. Scope & phase an MVP to control complexity
3. Wireframe & prototype sign up, rewards hub, and voucher redemption flows
4. Validate with shoppers and store associates, iterate with engineering
5. Build & launch pilot in Denmark → rollout to 12 more markets

## Key Features

- Unified dashboard - Consolidates orders, returns, and reward activity across web, app, and store.
- Offline capable member ID - Scannable QR / barcode that instantly recognises members at any POS, even without network connectivity.
- GDPR compliant sign up - Captures explicit marketing consent and offers a self serve preference centre.
- Near real time points ledger - Updates balances within x minutes of any transaction or return.
- Cross channel welcome perk - Automatic 10 % voucher redeemable on the first purchase online or in store.
- Fixed value reward vouchers - €5 / €10 / €20 vouchers unlocked at predefined point milestones, stackable per checkout.
