Tact.ai - Map feature for B2B Android App

Company: Tact.ai
Role: UX Design

Tact is a a personal sales assistant app built on Salesforce. While working here, I understood that designing for an enterprise product is very different than designing for consumer product. Business funcionality > Pixel design. I worked alongside the engineering manager and developers to build the map feature. Users can now see contacts, companies, and events in a map view. Users can also see which contacts are nearby with the 'nearby contacts' feature.

Cogent - Empowering admins manage client notifications

Company: Tact.ai
Role: UX Research/Design

The manual setting up of notifications in the backend resulted in many errors. Involving key stakeholders helped me understand the problem better. After several user interviews and iterative meetings, we figured out the personas and the technical challenges involved. I worked with the team to design a complete admin tool from groundup, which is now in beta.

My Story

I was always drawn to art and design from a very young age. I am inspired by my grandad, Late Shri. Prosenjit Dowerah, who was a pioneering force in Assam's art movement. I almost joined an engineering college to pursue B.Tech but my heart had other plans. I followed in my beloved gran's footsteps to pursue art but with a digital twist!

Post college, I landed my first job as a Game 2D/UI Artist with an agency. Then moved on to be a graphic designer with an ecommerce startup where I wore several hats. To further explore design, I moved on to UI/UX with tech startups. I enjoy working in agile teams.

So far, I've had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of things from logos, branding and marketing materials, social media banners, ad banners, landing pages, illustrations, ui/ux, developing content to later hiring potential candidates and mentoring interns. I am great with color theory, typography, conceptualization, product illustrations and all things visual design. I'm fairly new into UX, but I enjoy solving problems and strive to understand development issues and the best way out to fix a problem.

Apart from design, I love to read, jam with friends, meet new people, write poems, learn new languages and try different cuisines. I've recently moved to the Netherlands, hence learning Dutch now.
